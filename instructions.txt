Avatary Image Generator v1

Usage Instructions:

bash /install.sh
   
Runs one-time/full setup: installs or updates ComfyUI, installs required custom nodes and models, 
enables Manager UI, then starts ComfyUI.

bash /restart-comfyui.sh

Restarts the ComfyUI service only (stop/start + health check). It does not reinstall models or custom nodes.
